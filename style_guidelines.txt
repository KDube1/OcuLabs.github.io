Style of OcuLabs Website
------------------------

Overall
---
Our website will obey the current design standards for advertising websites
and service websites. Simple but effective.

Colours
---
#A0CCD2
- pastel blue
#040404
- Rich black
#13505B
- midnight green
#0C7489
- metallic seaweed
#119DA4
- blue-green

Iconography
---
Minimalist icons. We will use the icons from font awesome.

Typography
---
We will use the Helvetica font.

Links
---
There will be no visible hyperlinks on the site. They will all be buttons or
images.

Buttons
---
Our buttons will be darker than the background. When the user hovers over the
button it will become darker. The same applies to our navigation bar. The 
button for the active page will remain dark.
