Style of OcuLabs Website
------------------------

Overall
---
Our website will obey the current design standards for advertising websites
and service websites.

Colours
---
#A0CCD2
- pastel blue
#040404
- Rich black
#13505B
- midnight green
#0C7489
- metallic seaweed
#119DA4
- blue-green

Iconography
---
Minimalist icons. We will use the icons from font awesome.

Typography
---
We will use the Helvetica font.

Links
---
There will be no visible hyperlinks on the site. They will all be buttons or
images.

Buttons
---
Our buttons will be slightly transparent. When the user hovers over or clicks 
on the button it will become 100% opaque. The Active page in the nav bar will 
remain opaque. The transition will be instant.
